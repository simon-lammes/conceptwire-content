In my Angular application, I have this nice box component and on different pages I would like to put different content into that box. For example, on the pizza page, I would like to put pizza images inside. But I want to build the component in a flexible way so that I could put anything inside it. For which term should I look on the internet to find out how it works in Angular?
Angular refers to this concept as Content Projection.
In fact, this example calls for most simple form of content projection: single-slot content projection. In that case, a component just offers one place (aka. slot) that content can be projected to.
